# Acme Corporation Q4 2024 Financial Report

## Executive Summary

Acme Corporation delivered exceptional results in Q4 2024, with total revenue reaching $890 million, a 28% increase year-over-year. Our strategic investments in AI and automation drove significant margin improvements.

## Financial Highlights

### Revenue Breakdown
- Product Sales: $520 million (58% of total)
- Services: $280 million (31% of total)
- Licensing: $90 million (11% of total)

### Regional Performance
- Americas: $490 million (55%)
- EMEA: $267 million (30%)
- Asia-Pacific: $133 million (15%)

### Profitability
- Gross margin: 42%, up from 38% in Q4 2023
- Operating margin: 18%
- Net income: $142 million
- Earnings per share: $2.84

## Operational Metrics

### Workforce
Acme ended Q4 with 8,200 employees globally:
- Engineering: 3,100 (38%)
- Sales & Marketing: 2,050 (25%)
- Operations: 1,640 (20%)
- G&A: 1,410 (17%)

We hired 450 new employees in Q4, with a focus on AI/ML engineers and cloud architects.

### Customer Metrics
- Total customers: 12,400
- Enterprise customers (>$100K ARR): 890
- Net revenue retention: 118%
- Customer acquisition cost: $4,200

## Product Updates

### Acme Platform 3.0
Released in October 2024, featuring:
- Real-time analytics dashboard
- AI-powered recommendations
- Multi-cloud deployment support
- SOC 2 Type II certification

### Acme AI Assistant
Beta launched in December 2024:
- Natural language query interface
- Automated report generation
- Integration with 50+ enterprise tools

## 2025 Outlook

We project Q1 2025 revenue between $910 million and $940 million, representing 22-26% year-over-year growth. Full year 2025 guidance is $3.9 billion to $4.1 billion.

Key initiatives:
- Expand APAC presence with new Singapore office
- Launch Acme Platform 4.0 with enhanced AI capabilities
- Achieve FedRAMP authorization
- Target 10,000 employees by year-end
